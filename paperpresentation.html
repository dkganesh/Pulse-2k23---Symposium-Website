PAPER PRESENTATION

Description

Paper presentation is an event where the participants are required to make a paper about a certain topic or area, and then present it in front of a jury, following which it will be rated and judged based on their performance.

Domains (not restricted)

  • Electric Vehicle
  • Embedded Systems
  • Electrical Machines
  • Power Electronics
  • Power System
  • Nano Technology
  • Artificial Intelligence
  • Internet of Things(IoT)
  • Drones and Robotic Process Automation
  • Technology in Healthcare
  • Waste Management
  • Blockchain

Event Structure

Each paper presentation will be a maximum for a 12 minutes in length and will be followed by a brief question and answer period of approximately 3 minutes.

Rules And Regulations

  • Event consists of 1 round.
  • Team of 2-3 members will be permited.
  • Abstract is to be submitted, and should not exceed more than 250 words.
