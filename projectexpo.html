PROJECT EXPO

Description

The purpose of this event is to give the students an opportunity to unleash their potential and showcase their innovative projects before the jury. Thus, with this event its a unique chance for the participants to gain exposure and also to depict their learning experience.

Rules And Regulations

  • Maximum of 3 participants per team
  • Five minutes will be given for each to present their project to the jury.
  • PPT presentation is NOT a must.
  • Stand up model, a working model or a product should be shown.
  • The event consists of only one round.
