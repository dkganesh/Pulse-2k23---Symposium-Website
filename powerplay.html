POWER SYS SIM

Description

In the first round of quiz, fundamental questions in Power systems will be given. In the second round, the questions will be related Power System Simulation and applications.

Event Structure

  • Round 1

    Quiz(25 Questions)

  • Round 2

    Quiz(10 Questions)

Rules And Regulations

  • Maximum-2 members per team.
  • Time limit applies to each round.
  • Round 1 is a technical quiz consist of 25 questions and top 5 teams will be selected for Round 2.
  • Round 2 is also a technical quiz consist of 10 questions and winners will be decided from this round.
