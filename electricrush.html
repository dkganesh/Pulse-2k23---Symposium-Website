ELECTRIC RUSH

Description

Race against the clock, solve the problems given to you while overcoming the challenges given in the middle. Solve them to gain time.

Rules And Regulations

  • A team of two will be working on a problem under limited time.
  • Solving the challenges given in the middle will buy some time.
  • Use of mobile phones is strictly prohibited.
  • The first to finish will be the victors.
