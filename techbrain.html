TECHBRAIN

Description

Participants should talk ‘for and against’ about the given theme. Presenting valid key points despite frequent switching of perspective. Participants must justify their points if asked by the host or the audience.

Event Structure

All participants will be given 10 minutes for preparation and 15 minutes to speak and another 5 minutes to ask questions to the participant by the audience. Participants should stand on the stage and start with ‘for or against’ the topic as told by the host. They will be interrupted by the host or the audience to justify the point. If they are unable to justify their point, Score will not be given . Participants who give more valid points within the given time will be the Winner.
