CIRCUIT SENTRY

Description

This event is about exploring the theoretical and practical knowledge about Electric Circuits. In order to brush up your cells, we’ll start off with a technical quiz round, which is also an elimination round. The top 7 will be allowed to move onto the next round, which is to bring out the circuit designer in you!!!

Domains

  • Linear Integrated Circuits
  • Power Electronics

Event Structure

Round 1: Quiz
Round 2: Working with physical circuits

Rules And Regulations

  • Maximum of 2-3 members in a team.
  • Time limits apply to each round.
  • Round 1 - elimination round that consists of a technical quiz, The top 7 team finishers progress to Round 2.
  • Round 2 - one team must assemble the given electronics components to form a desired circuit within the allotted time.
